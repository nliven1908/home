HYPERPOETRY

This project is an initial attempt to bridge the philosophical thought of ‘Continuum Theory & Hausdorff Space’ along with the concept of Hypertext through the use of poems that have the subject matter of ‘infinity’ as its core. Poetry of various cultural traditions and forms have been used in an interactive design base. The user has to find patterns to generate poems from the variegated data.

Level 1.0

Type

  • Infinite attention based cognitive game
  • Philosophical rendition of Hausdorff Space where the player has to continue to generate the poems by observation and putting together the pieces of his/her own next choice to complete a logical poem-ic syntax. 

Project Headlines

  • Inauguration of patent filing in tandem under the supervision of the IIT Gandhinagar incubation cell.
  • Launched at the Digital Humanities Alliance of India (DHAI) conference 2018.
